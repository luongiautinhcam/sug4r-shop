# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: February 2026_

## Information We Collect

- **Email address**: Required to process orders and deliver products.
- **IP address**: Collected for security and fraud prevention. Retained for 90 days.
- **Order information**: Products purchased, payment status, and delivery status.

## How We Use Your Information

- To process and fulfill your orders.
- To communicate order status and delivery links.
- To detect and prevent fraud and abuse.
- To comply with legal obligations.

## What We Do NOT Collect

- We do not require account registration.
- We do not use third-party tracking or analytics cookies.
- We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties.

## Data Security

- All sensitive data (credentials, payment information) is encrypted at rest.
- We use HTTPS for all communications.
- Admin access is protected by strong authentication and audit logging.

## Data Retention

- Order data is retained for accounting and legal purposes.
- Security logs (IP addresses) are retained for 90 days.
- You may request deletion of your personal data by contacting us.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data (via order lookup).
- Request correction or deletion of your data.
- Withdraw consent for data processing.

## Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, please contact us through the information provided on the website.
